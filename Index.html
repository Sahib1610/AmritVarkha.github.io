Gurbani (Punjabi: ਗੁਰਬਾਣੀ) is a Sikh term,
very commonly used by Sikhs to refer to various compositions
by the Sikh Gurus and other writers of Guru Granth Sahib.
In general, hymns in the central text of the Sikhs,
the Guru Granth Sahib, are called Gurbani.
